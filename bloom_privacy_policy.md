# Privacy Policy

**App Name:** Bloom – Your Pregnancy Companion
**Effective Date:** June 10, 2025
**Last Updated:** June 10, 2025

---

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Bloom ("we", "our", or "us"). Bloom is a pregnancy tracking app designed to support you through every step of your pregnancy journey. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with care, transparency, and respect.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Bloom, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you create an account or use Bloom, you may provide:

- **Account information** — your name, email address, and password
- **Pregnancy information** — your due date, last menstrual period (LMP) date, baby's name (optional), and baby's gender preference (optional)
- **Health tracking data** — kick counts, contraction records, weight logs, and appointment details
- **Photos** — bump diary photos you choose to upload
- **Planning data** — hospital bag checklists, birth plans, to-do lists, and shopping lists
- **Chat messages** — conversations with our AI pregnancy assistant
- **Profile photo** — if you choose to upload one

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use Bloom, we automatically collect:

- **Device information** — device type, operating system version, and app version
- **Usage data** — features you use, screens you visit, and time spent in the app
- **Crash reports** — technical error logs to help us fix bugs and improve the app

### 2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

- Your precise GPS location
- Contacts from your phone
- Microphone or camera data without your explicit action
- Data from other apps on your device
- Financial or payment information

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- **Provide the app's features** — calculate your pregnancy week, show baby development content, and personalize your experience
- **Power the AI assistant** — your messages are sent to our AI service to generate pregnancy-related responses
- **Send notifications** — appointment reminders, weekly pregnancy updates, and daily tips (only if you enable them)
- **Improve Bloom** — analyze usage patterns to fix bugs and build better features
- **Ensure security** — detect and prevent unauthorized access to your account

We do **not** use your data for advertising, and we do **not** sell your data to any third party.

---

## 4. How We Share Your Information

We take your privacy seriously. We only share your data in the following limited circumstances:
 

### 4.1 Partner Access (Optional)

If you choose to share your pregnancy progress with a partner using the Partner Code feature, they will have read-only access to your dashboard information. You can revoke this access at any time from Settings.

### 4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government authority, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the safety of any person.



---

## 5. Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored on **Supabase** servers with industry-standard encryption (AES-256 at rest, TLS in transit)
- We use **Row Level Security (RLS)** — meaning your data is technically isolated from other users at the database level
- Passwords are never stored in plain text — they are hashed using secure algorithms
- We regularly review our security practices to keep your data safe

However, no system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong password and keep your account credentials private.

---

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

### 6.1 Access and Correction
You can view and edit your profile information at any time from **Settings → Edit Profile**.

### 6.2 Data Export
You can request a copy of all your data by contacting us at the email below. We will respond within 30 days.

### 6.3 Data Deletion
You can permanently delete your account and all associated data from **Settings → Delete Account**. This action is irreversible. We will delete your data within 30 days of your request.

### 6.4 Notifications
You can turn off push notifications at any time from **Settings → Notifications** or from your device's system settings.



---

## 7. Children's Privacy

Bloom is intended for use by adults (18 years and older) who are pregnant or supporting someone who is pregnant. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately. If you believe a child has used our app, please contact us.

---

## 8. Sensitive Health Data

We understand that pregnancy information is deeply personal and sensitive. We treat all health-related data (due dates, weight logs, kick counts, birth plans, etc.) with extra care:

- This data is **never shared** with advertisers or data brokers
- This data is **never used** to target you with ads inside or outside the app
- This data is **only accessible** to you and the service providers listed in Section 4
- You can **delete all health data** by deleting your account

---

## 9. AI Chatbot (Bloom Assistant)

Our AI pregnancy assistant is powered by Anthropic's Claude AI. When you use the chatbot:

- Your messages are sent to Anthropic's API to generate responses
- Anthropic may process these messages per their own privacy policy (anthropic.com/privacy)
- We store your chat history in our database so you can refer back to previous conversations
- You can delete your chat history at any time from the Chat screen

**Important:** The AI assistant provides general information only and is not a substitute for professional medical advice. Always consult your doctor or midwife for personal medical concerns.

---

## 10. Bump Diary Photos

Photos you upload to the Bump Diary feature:

- Are stored securely in our private Supabase Storage bucket
- Are only accessible to you (and your partner if you have enabled Partner Sharing)
- Are never used for training AI models
- Are never shared publicly or with third parties
- Are permanently deleted when you delete your account
