03 — Data Use

How We Use Your Information

We use your information only for the following purposes. We do not use your personal data for advertising profiling or to build marketing audiences.

  • To provide the App's core features — food diary, calorie tracking, workout logging, progress charts, and habit tracking
  • To personalise your experience — calculating your daily calorie goal, macro targets, BMI, and recommended intake based on your body information and goals
  • To sync your data — keeping your information consistent across your devices and backing it up securely
  • To send you notifications — meal reminders, workout alerts, and habit check-ins, only if you enable them in Settings
  • To improve the App — analysing anonymised, aggregated usage patterns to understand which features are most useful and where we can do better
  • To process payments — managing your subscription and verifying purchase status through our payment partner
  • To provide customer support — responding to your questions, feedback, and support requests
  • To ensure safety and security — detecting abuse, fraud, and technical vulnerabilities
  • To meet legal obligations — complying with applicable laws and regulations
04 — Sharing

Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or data brokers.

We share information only in the limited circumstances below:

  • Service providers — We work with trusted companies that help us operate the App, including cloud hosting, payment processing, and analytics. These partners process data strictly on our behalf under confidentiality agreements and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.
  • Legal requirements — We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or a governmental authority, or to protect the rights and safety of our users and the public.
  • Business transfers — If FitPro is acquired or merges with another company, your data may transfer to the new owner, who will be bound by the same privacy protections described here. We will notify you before any such transfer takes effect.
  • With your consent — In any other situation, we will only share your information with your explicit prior consent.
We never share your health, fitness, or dietary data with employers, insurers, advertisers, or any commercial third party for their own use.
05 — Third Parties

Third-Party Services

To deliver its features, FitPro relies on a number of specialist service providers. Each provider only receives the minimum data necessary to perform their function, and each operates under its own privacy policy.

The categories of service providers we use are:

  • Authentication & account services — Secure sign-in, identity verification, and account management
  • Cloud storage & database services — Secure storage and synchronisation of your diary, workout, and progress data
  • Nutrition data providers — Food nutrition databases that power the food search and diary features. Only your search query text is sent — no personal information is transmitted.
  • AI recognition services — When you use the AI food photo feature (Premium), your food photo is sent to an AI service solely to identify the food and estimate its nutrition. The image is not stored by us or the AI provider after processing.
  • Payment & subscription processing — Handling in-app purchases and subscription verification on iOS and Android. We never see or store your payment card details.
  • Analytics services — Anonymised, aggregated data about how the App is used, to help us improve features. No personally identifiable information is included.
  • Crash reporting & performance monitoring — Automated error logs that help us identify and fix technical problems quickly.
  • Push notification delivery — Sending reminders and alerts to your device when you have notifications enabled.
You can request a full list of our current service providers by contacting us at privacy@FitPro.app. We are transparent about who we work with upon request.
06 — Health Data

Health & Fitness Data

Health and fitness information is among the most sensitive data we handle. We apply stricter protections to this category above and beyond our standard data practices.

Your health data is never used for advertising. It is never shared with insurers, employers, or marketing companies. It is used solely to operate FitPro for you personally.
  • All health and fitness data is encrypted during storage and while in transit between your device and our servers
  • Your food diary, workout history, body measurements, and progress photos are accessible only by you and are not visible to other users
  • If you choose to connect Apple HealthKit or Google Health Connect, we access only the specific data types you explicitly authorise. You can revoke this permission at any time in your device Settings.
  • Data from Apple HealthKit is not used for advertising, marketing, or sold to third parties — in compliance with Apple's HealthKit guidelines
  • Progress photos are stored in a private area of our storage system and are not accessible to any other user or FitPro staff except for essential technical maintenance
  • You can permanently delete all your health data at any time from Settings → Account → Delete My Data
07 — Camera

Camera Use

FitPro requests access to your device camera for two specific purposes only. We never access your camera in the background or without a deliberate action on your part.

  • Barcode scanning — When you scan a packaged food item, the barcode number is sent to a nutrition database to retrieve product information. No image is captured, stored, or transmitted — only the barcode number.
  • AI food photo recognition (Premium) — When you choose to photograph a meal, the image is sent over an encrypted connection to an AI service that identifies the food and estimates its nutritional content. Neither we nor the AI service store the image after it has been processed.
  • Progress photos (Premium) — Photos you voluntarily upload to track your visual progress are stored securely in your private account and are never shared or used for any other purpose.
You can revoke camera permission at any time in your device's Settings app. Revoking camera access will disable barcode scanning and AI food photo features but will not affect manual food search or any other App functionality.

09 — Your Rights

Your Rights

You have meaningful rights over your personal data. Most of these can be exercised directly within the App, and all can be exercised by contacting us.

  • Right to access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you at any time
  • Right to correction — Update or correct any inaccurate information from your Profile screen at any time
  • Right to deletion — Permanently delete your account and all associated data via Settings → Account → Delete My Account
  • Right to data portability — Export your food logs, workout history, and progress records (Premium)
  • Right to restrict processing — Request that we limit how we use your data in certain circumstances
  • Right to object — Object to the processing of your data for direct marketing purposes at any time
  • Right to withdraw consent — Withdraw any consent you have given at any time, without affecting any processing that occurred before withdrawal
  • Right to lodge a complaint — File a complaint with your national data protection authority. EU/UK residents may contact their respective supervisory authority.
California residents (CCPA): You have the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to request deletion, and the right to opt-out of sale — we do not sell your data. You also have the right to non-discrimination for exercising any of these rights.
10 — Children

Children's Privacy

FitPro is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or under 16 in the European Union). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children below these ages.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has created an account or provided personal information without your consent, please contact us immediately at privacy@FitPro.app. We will investigate and delete the information promptly upon verification.

Users between the ages of 13 and 18 may use the App only with the consent and supervision of a parent or legal guardian.

11 — Security

Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously and implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, alteration, or disclosure.

  • All data transmitted between the App and our servers is protected using strong, industry-standard encryption in transit
  • Your data is stored using encryption at rest on our secure cloud infrastructure
  • Access to your personal data within our systems is restricted on a strict need-to-know basis
  • Your password is never stored by us — sign-in is handled through a dedicated, secure authentication service
  • Your personal data in our database is protected by security rules that ensure only your own account can access your information
  • We regularly review and test our security practices and update our systems to address emerging threats
  • Two-factor authentication is available for your account under Settings → Security
No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we use our best efforts to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security. In the event of a data breach that affects your rights, we will notify you and relevant authorities as required by applicable law.
